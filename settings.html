Group Project 1

steps to take moving forward for projects. Your project presentations will be on August 17th

1. please make a channel for your group where all your communication will go.
2. come up with your idea, it must use firebase, html, css, bootstrap or another css framework, jquery, at least 2 APIs
3. get your idea approved by me - you cannot continue until you get your idea approved
4. show with code that the apis you attend to work with work - get this approved by me - you cannot continue until you show that your apis work
5. make a wireframe (sketch of all of the pages and how they work with each other) - get this approved by me - you cannot continue until you show your wireframe
6. make a task list on trello.com - you must get this approved by me
7. start coding
